1. Identify the limiting reagent in the reaction 2A + 4B → 3C + 4D, when 5 moles of A react with 6 moles of B. (2025 March)
Answer: B is the limiting reagent (For 5 moles of A, 10 moles of B are required, but only 6 moles are available.)
2. Define the law of multiple proportions. Explain with one example. (2025 March)
When two elements combine to form more than one compound, the masses of one element that combine with a fixed mass of the other element are in the ratio of small whole numbers. Example: H₂O and H₂O₂, CO and CO₂.
